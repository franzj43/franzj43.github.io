I'm a Data Engineer with over 4 years of experience building data infrastructure that powers analytics, machine learning, and operational intelligence. My background in Chemical Engineering — earned Cum Laude — gives me an uncommon foundation: I approach data systems like process engineering problems, obsessing over throughput, reliability, and failure modes.

I've worked across industries — finance, environmental services, consumer goods — designing ETL/ELT pipelines, dimensional data models, and near-real-time ingestion systems.
